\n## 2026-05-06 - ARIA roles for button-based selection arrays\n**Learning:** When using a group of buttons to act as a custom radio input selection (e.g., selecting service types), standard buttons and visual cues are insufficient for screen readers. Using `role="radiogroup"` on the container with an `aria-labelledby` attribute, and `role="radio"` with `aria-checked` on the individual buttons ensures the component is properly interpreted as a single-choice input.\n**Action:** Always implement `role="radiogroup"` and `role="radio"` with appropriate states (`aria-checked`) and `focus-visible` utility classes for any custom button-based selection arrays to maintain robust keyboard and screen reader accessibility.
